## Scope

This traceability record maps the beginner-friendly Modern Robotics bridge in `paper/sections/05b_robotics_foundations.tex` to later impedance-control explanations, MuJoCo lab interpretation, citations, and validation gates.

The goal is not to prove the simulator implements all robotics theory. The goal is to make sure every major tutorial concept introduced in Section 5b has:

- a local explanation or equation in Section 5b,
- a citation, derivation, or explicit educational caveat,
- a later anchor in Section 6 or Section 7,
- a measurable validation or review gate,
- a next action when the evidence is weak.
